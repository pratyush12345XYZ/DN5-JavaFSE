Exercise 5: Triggers

Scenario 1:
The UpdateCustomerLastModified trigger automatically updates the LastModified column whenever a customer's record is updated. This helps maintain an accurate record of the last modification made to customer information.

Scenario 2:
The LogTransaction trigger automatically inserts a record into the AuditLog table whenever a new transaction is added to the Transactions table. This maintains an audit trail of all banking transactions for security and monitoring purposes.

Scenario 3:
The CheckTransactionRules trigger enforces business rules before a transaction is inserted. It prevents deposits with non-positive amounts and blocks withdrawals that exceed the available account balance, thereby maintaining data integrity and preventing invalid transactions.
